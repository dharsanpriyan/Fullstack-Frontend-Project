Rameshwaram

Rameshwaram is a city in the Indian state of Tamil Nadu, which is a renowned tourist destination. It is located on the Pamban island and is considered to be a sacred Hindu pilgrim destination. Famous for the Ramayana tales, this wonderful city is surrounded by water from four sides and creates a beautiful scenery in the backdrop.
